# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-04-20**

Librarium for iOS is a client application for a self-hosted Librarium server
that you (or someone you trust) operates. The app exists to let you browse and
manage a personal book, manga, and audiobook library that lives on that
server — nowhere else.

## What data the app collects

**None, as far as the developer is concerned.** The app does not send any
personal information, telemetry, analytics, advertising identifiers, crash
reports, or usage metrics to the developer or to any third party.

## What data the app transmits

The app sends network requests only to the Librarium server URL you configure
inside the app. Those requests carry:

- Your authentication credentials, as required to log in to your own server
- Library data you view, edit, or add (book titles, ISBNs, reading progress,
  notes you type, cover images, etc.)
- Standard HTTP metadata (request timestamps, user-agent string)

All of this travels between your iOS device and the server you chose. The
developer operates no servers on your behalf and has no access to this data.

## Third-party services

The app does not embed any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising,
tracking, attribution, or crash reporting. Apple's standard platform services
(App Store, TestFlight, iCloud if you enable it at the OS level) are governed
by Apple's own privacy terms, not this policy.

## Data retention

All library data is retained by your Librarium server according to whatever
retention policy its operator applies. When you delete the app from your
device, any local cache is removed with it. The developer retains nothing.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
any data from anyone. See above — it collects nothing from any user.
